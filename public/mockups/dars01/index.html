Is WorkWorld Right for you?

This current version is designed for Virginians* with disabilities who receive or are applying for any or all of the following benefits:

  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI)
  • Medicaid
  • Medicare

*In the future, WW-Web will include more benefit programs and w ill help residents of other states.

Benefits from WorkWorld

Explore ways to increase your net income through employment or selfemployment.

Find out what will happen to your benefits if you earn different amounts and if you use the various “work incentives” that the benefit programs provide for you.

Find a safe path toward more financial independence, a path in which you keep the benefits you need until you can reduce or eliminate your need for those benefits.
