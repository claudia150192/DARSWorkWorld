Question - Spouse Disability-Related Work Expenses

Enter the actual monthly amount of your spouse’s disability-related work expenses, in dollars and cents.

If your spouse goes to work there may be expenses that you wouldn't have if your spouse weren't working. If any of your spouse’s work expenses are caused by your spouse’s disability, enter them here to see what effect doing so might have on your spouse’s Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) eligibility and/or Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefit amount.

Additional questions will be asked later to help determine how best to apply these expenses.

The effects of claiming disability=related work expenses are quite different in these two programs. Read more about them by following the additional information link below.
