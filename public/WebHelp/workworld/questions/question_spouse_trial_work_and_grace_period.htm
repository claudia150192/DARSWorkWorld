Question - Spouse Trial Work and Grace Period

Select “Yes” if your spouse has completed the Trial Work Period (TWP) and Grace Period, or “No” if your spouse has not.

The Trial Work Period begins the first month in which your spouse earns the “services” amount or works more than 80 self-employed hours. It continues until your spouse accumulates 9 months in which your spouse performs at the services level within a rolling 60-month period. Once your spouse completes the TWP, the 36-month Extended Period of Eligibility begins. If earnings exceed Substantial Gainful Activity, the 3-month Grace Period begins.
