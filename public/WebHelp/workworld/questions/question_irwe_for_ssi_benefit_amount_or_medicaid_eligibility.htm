Question - IRWE for SSI Benefit Amount or Medicaid Eligibility

Enter the actual monthly amount of your disability-related work expenses that are counted as Impairment Related Work Expenses (IRWE) to determine Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefit amount and/or Medicaid eligibility, in dollars and cents.

The question displays the amount of your disability-related work expenses that you provided in a previous question.

The IRWE amount you enter in this question can not exceed the disability related work expenses amount.
