Question - Spouse Blind Work Expenses (BWE)

Enter the actual monthly amount of your spouse’s disability-related work expenses, estimated work-related taxes, and other work expenses that are counted as Blind Work Expenses (BWE), in dollars and cents.

The question displays the amounts of your spouse’s disability-related work expenses, estimated work-related taxes, and other work expenses that you provided in previous questions.

The BWE amount you enter in this question can not exceed the sum of your spouse’s disability related work expenses, estimated work-related taxes, and other work expenses.
