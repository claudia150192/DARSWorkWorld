Question - Trial Work and Grace Period

Select “Yes” if you have completed the Trial Work Period (TWP) and Grace Period, or “No” if you have not.

The Trial Work Period begins the first month in which you earn the “services” amount or work more than 80 self-employed hours. It continues until you accumulate 9 months in which you perform at the services level within a rolling 60-month period. Once you complete the TWP, the 36-month Extended Period of Eligibility begins. If earnings exceed Substantial Gainful Activity, the 3-month Grace Period begins.
