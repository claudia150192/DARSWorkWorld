Question - Situation Name

Enter a brief label to help you remember the change(s) you make for each "What-If" situation. The labels will then appear as headings on various pages and for the columns in the input grid and in the text and numerical results output grids. The labels will also display in graphical output screens. Finally, all printed output will show the labels.

An example of a label for the current situation might be, "Unemployed." An example of a "What-If" might be, "Earn $750 per Month". You can see how entering the labels enables you to visualize what each situation represents, rather than relying on your memory to keep track of what changed in the "What-If" situations.
