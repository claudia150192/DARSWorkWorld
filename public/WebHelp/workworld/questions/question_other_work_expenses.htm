Question - Other Work Expenses

Enter the actual monthly amount of your other, non-disability-related work expenses, in dollars and cents.

If you go to work you may have expenses that you wouldn't have if you weren't working. If you have any of these expenses (that are not caused by your disability), enter them here to see what effect doing so might have on your Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefit eligibility or amount.

Additional questions will be asked later to help determine how best to apply these expenses.
