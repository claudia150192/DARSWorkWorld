Question - Situation Date

Enter the date for this situation. You may enter it directly in the form MM/DD/YYYY, or use the calendar to select it.

WorkWORLD will use this date, in conjunction with your birthdate, to determine your age within each situation.

Various rates and limits change from time to time, and you can choose to check results for a previous or future date. The date cannot be earlier than the earliest date for rates contained in this version of WorkWORLD, and if you choose a future date you will receive estimated results based on the latest available rates.

In order to use this version of WorkWORLD, the combination of Birthdate and Situation Dates you enter must yield an age between 15 and 65.
