Question - Spouse Employability Investments

Enter the actual monthly amount your spouse is spending or setting aside to improve your spouse’s future financial self-sufficiency, in dollars and cents.

An “employability investment” is money your spouse spends (or saves to spend later) for training, education, transportation, equipment, software, or anything else that can be part of a plan to achieve an occupational goal that makes your spouse more employable or increases your spouse’s earnings.
