Question - Spouse Considered to be Community Spouse

Select “Yes” if your spouse is considered to be a “community spouse” for Medicaid purposes, or “No” if your spouse is not.

If you are in a facility or receive Home and Community Based Care (HBC) Waiver Services, then your spouse is considered a "community spouse" unless your spouse is in a facility. A spouse who is on a waiver can be a community spouse.
