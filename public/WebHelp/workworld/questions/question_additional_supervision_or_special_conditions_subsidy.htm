Question - Additional Supervision or Special Conditions Subsidy

Enter the dollar value of any subsidy you get for additional supervision or special conditions provided to you because of your disability, in dollars and cents.

These terms represent support you receive on the job that may result in receiving more pay than the actual value of services performed.

A subsidy is provided by the employer. Special conditions may be provided by the employer or someone else.
