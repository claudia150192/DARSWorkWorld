Question - Wages, Salary, Self-Employment Earned Amount

Enter the actual monthly amount you earn in gross wages, salary, and self-employment in dollars and cents.

For Social Security Disability Income (SSDI) purposes, these wages are counted as earnings for the month in which they are earned, regardless of when they are paid or received.
