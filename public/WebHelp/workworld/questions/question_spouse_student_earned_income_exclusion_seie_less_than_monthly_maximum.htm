Question - Spouse Student Earned Income Exclusion (SEIE) Less Than Monthly Maximum

Select “Yes” if your spouse has used up your spouse’s Student Earned Income Exclusion (SEIE) annual exclusion amount, and “No” if your spouse has not.

The question displays your spouse’s SEIE monthly exclusion amount you provided in a previous question and shows the monthly and annual maximum SEIE amounts.

You can include all of your spouse’s earnings in your spouse’s monthly SEIE amount up to the monthly SEIE limit as long as you do not exceed the annual SEIE limit in a calendar year.
