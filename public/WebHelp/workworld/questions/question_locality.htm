Question - Locality

Select the locality in which you reside from the list

Remember that in Virginia, cities are completely independent. They are not part of any adjacent or surrounding county.

Where you live (specifically, such factors as your area's mean and/or median income, population, poverty rate, unemployment rate, housing cost, designation by specific benefit programs, etc.) may affect the rules and/or rates of certain benefit programs. Each benefit program determines how to factor in differences between localities or regions in an attempt to provide benefits more fairly to all, regardless of where they live.
