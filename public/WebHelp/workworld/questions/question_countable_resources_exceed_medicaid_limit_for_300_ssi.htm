Question - Countable Resources Exceed Medicaid Limit for 300% SSI

Select “Yes” if your countable resources exceed the Medicaid limit for individuals (for the 300% Supplemental Security Income [SSI] covered group) shown in the question, or “No” if they do not.

An unmarried person who either gets or applies for 300% SSI Medicaid is subject to the individual resource limit. A married person who gets 300% SSI Medicaid is also treated as a unit of one. A married person who applies for 300% SSI Medicaid is treated as a unit of two.
