About WorkWORLD on the Web

This current version is designed for Virginians* with disabilities who receive or are applying for any or all of the following benefits:

·  Supplemental Security Income (SSI)

·  Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI)

·  Medicaid

·  Medicare

With WorkWORLD on the-Web you can explore ways to increase your net income through employment or self-employment. This tool will tell you what will happen to your benefits if you earn different amounts and if you use the various “work incentives” that the benefit programs provide for you. WW-Web can help you find a safe path toward more financial independence, a path in which you keep the benefits you need until you can reduce or eliminate your need for those benefits.

* In the future, WW-Web will include more benefit programs and will help residents of other states.
